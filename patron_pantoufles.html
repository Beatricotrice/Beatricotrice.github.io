Pantoufles

Créateur: Stephanie Zed
Difficulté: Avancé
Temps estimé : 5 à 6 heures
Laine: Chunky (heavy weight)
Taille de crochet suggérée: 6,0 mm
Dimensions : 20 cm x 10 cm x 10 cm

Legend
Rd : round
Ch : chain
DC : double crochet
SS : slip stitch
[...] X3 : repeat the sequence 3 times
FpDc : front post double crochet
BpDc : back post double crochet

Sole & Top

Measure your foot from the toes to the arch. Keep repeating round 12 until your work has that length. (I typically work from round 4 to 12.)

Cut the yarn leaving about 25 cm and pull through.

Turn the slipper inside out. Pull the centre circle snug and tie off end.

Place heel together matching up the stitches. Darn together. Go through top of stitches

Turn the slipper right side out

Ankle

Cut yarn and pull through. Close with an invisible stitch.

Tie off the ends.
