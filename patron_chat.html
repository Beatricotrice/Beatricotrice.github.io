Chaton

Créateur: Stella's Yarn Universe
Difficulté: Intermédiaire
Temps estimé : 2 à 3 heures
Laine: Light
Taille de crochet suggérée: 2,5 mm
Dimensions : 2 cm x 2 cm x 6 cm

Legend
Rd : round
SC : single crochet
INC : increase (2 SC in the same stitch)
[...] X3 : repeat the sequence 3 times
DEC : decrease
Ch: chain
BLO : back loops only
Sk : skip
HDC : half double crochet
DC : double crochet

Head & Body

Embroider the eyes, nose, mouth and whiskers.
 

Stuff.
 

Fasten off leaving a long tail. Insert tail end in FLO of all 6 st, then stitch through the middle of the last rd to the spot above the cat’s hind leg between rd 13 and 14, creating a shallow cavity in the bottom, that will help the cat sit.

Now use the yarn end to shape the legs by stitching through the body above the hind legs, in between hind and forelegs, as well as between the forelegs, pulling the yarn tight enough to create some tension.

Ears (X2)

Fasten off leaving a long enough end to sew on the ear. Slightly bend the ears and sew them on rd 2 to 4 on each side of the head.
